Company Profile

Established in 2017, Wisdoer Global is a HRD Corp Registered Training Provider. We specialize in delivering comprehensive training programs using our founder Elijah Ting's proprietary Strategic Dialogue™ framework to turn soft skills into your company's most powerful strategic assets.
